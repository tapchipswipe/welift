## 4. Overnight opportunity (8pm–6am)

### Why 8pm–6am is the beachhead near 34211

1. Targets the **lowest-utilization booth hours** without taking on 5–8pm or 6–9am rush traffic.
2. Leaves evening/morning for an optional on-site guard + existing LiftMaster codes (easy hybrid sell).
3. Boards already discuss virtual / hybrid gates (LWR public record).
4. Easier staffing and SLA than a 5pm–9am product.

- LiftMaster keypad stays live; your attendants take intercom exceptions 8pm–6am
