## Named competitors — detail

### 1. Envera Systems (primary pure-play rival)

**Public stance:** Custom quotes; positions vs **$14k–$22k/mo** manned posts.

**Documented contract (Del Webb Oak Creek HOA — public DocuSign packet):**

| Line item | Monthly |
|-----------|---------|
| Envera Kiosk (Envera-owned) + DL automation | **$750** |
| Per-home monitoring 12 hrs (7pm–7am) | **$3.00 / home** (shown $0 when qty 0 in sample) |
| Service & maintenance (kiosk) | **~$211** |
| Database management (readers) | **$250** |
| Other maintenance lines | **~$150** |
| **Sample total on that exhibit** | **~$1,360**/mo |

**Other published signals:**
- Competitor (Tap2Open) claims Envera often **~$11+/home/mo** all-in service  
  - 100 homes → **~$1,100+/mo**; 300 homes → **~$3,300+/mo**
- Proptia case: one community quoted **~$70,644/yr (~$5,887/mo)** including cameras/construction lease vs manned alternatives

**Takeaway vs you:** Envera often **bundles hardware lease + monitoring**. Your wedge is **service-only on existing myQ** at overnight hours — can undercut full Envera stacks on sites that already have LiftMaster.

### 2. Allied Universal (local SWFL incumbent)

- **Does not publish** virtual-gate rate cards.
- **Lakewood Ranch (2020 Observer):** Allied tech/virtual gatehouse proposal **$295,680/yr**; Envera bid **$182,141**; others **$261k–$322k** (multi-gate / multi-CDD scope — not 1 HOA overnight).
- Separate **manned** Allied HOA contracts elsewhere show **~$20k–$24k+/mo** for full welcome-gate staffing (e.g. Heritage Landing-style personnel agreements).

**Takeaway:** Allied wins hybrid RFPs; price is **project-sized**. You compete on **small HOA overnight + existing myQ**, not multi-million CDD tech packages.

### 3. Virtual Guard (V-Gates) — markets Sarasota County

- No public HOA rate card found.
- Claims up to **~70% savings** vs on-site.
- Expect similar ballpark to Envera: **low thousands / month** + hardware.

## Price positioning for your offer

| Buyer invoice today | Your $4,800 overnight | Competitive read |
|---------------------|----------------------|------------------|
| Night booth ~$9,100/mo | Save ~47% | Strong vs labor |
| Envera-style ~$1,300–$3,500/mo (small/mid) | You may be **higher** | Need clearer SLA / local / myQ-no-rip story **or** price down to ~$2.5–$4k |
| Envera ~$5,000–$6,000/mo bundled | You can win on **no new kiosk lease** | Emphasize existing LiftMaster/myQ |
| Allied multi-gate RFP $15k–$25k+/mo | Different buyer | Don’t bid that scope yet |

**Pricing recommendation:**
- Keep **$4,800** if selling against **manned overnight**.
- Have a **$2,900–$3,900** “myQ already installed” rate when the alternative is Envera add-on monitoring only.
- Never race ButterflyMX on $/door — different product.
