# Channel Test — CAM Interview Guide

**Goal:** Validate willingness of Sarasota–Bradenton–Lakewood Ranch property managers to replace or hybridize overnight gate labor with live video virtual attendants.

**Target:** 5 conversations (15–25 min each). Prefer community association managers (CAMs) or vendor managers at the firms in `target-cam-firms.md`.

---

## Opening (30 seconds)

> “I’m building a local overnight virtual gate attendant service for gated HOAs around Lakewood Ranch / Sarasota — live video agents who verify guests and open the gate remotely, similar to what larger vendors pitch, but local. I’m not selling today; I want 20 minutes on how boards actually buy gate security and what’s broken.”

---

## Questions (ask in order; skip if time-boxed)

### Current state
1. Roughly how many of your gated associations still have **manned overnight** gates vs keypad / phone entry only?
2. What’s the typical **monthly** spend for overnight gate coverage on a mid-size community?
3. Top 3 complaints from boards or residents about gate vendors in the last year?

### Appetite for virtual / hybrid
4. Have any of your boards evaluated **virtual gate / remote attendant** (Allied, Envera, Virtual Guard, etc.)? What happened?
5. Would **overnight-only virtual** (keep daytime booth if they want) be an easier board sell than full replacement?
6. What would make a board say no immediately? (liability, no physical presence, CapEx, incumbent contract term, resident politics)

### Buying process
7. Who initiates vendor change — CAM recommendation, board president, CDD, or RFP?
8. Typical decision cycle length?
9. Do you need preferred-vendor status / COIs / Class B before you’ll even intro a board?

### Willingness signals
10. On a 1–5 scale, how open are you to piloting overnight virtual on **one** association in the next 6 months if pricing saves ≥30% and SLAs are written?
11. What proof would you need first (local reference, 30-day soft pilot, insurance limits, board packet)?

### Closing
12. Who else locally should I talk to (another CAM, access-control installer, CDD ops)?
13. OK to send a one-page overnight offer for you to keep on file?
