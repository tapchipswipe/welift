## 🤝 HOA Value Proposition vs. Human Guards

* **Overnight Human Guard Booth:** Costs HOAs **$109,500 / year** ($30.00/hour from 8pm–6am).
* **24/7 Human Guard Booth:** Costs HOAs **$306,600 / year** ($35.00/hour 24/7).
* **We Lift @ $1.00/call:** Costs HOA **$12,000 / year** -> **HOA saves $97,500 / year (89.0% savings)**.
* **We Lift @ $2.00/call:** Costs HOA **$24,000 / year** -> **HOA saves $85,500 / year (78.0% savings)**.
* **We Lift @ $3.00/call:** Costs HOA **$36,000 / year** -> **HOA saves $73,500 / year (67.1% savings)**.

*Key Takeaway:* Even at $3.00 per call, the HOA board saves over $73k every year, while you capture a **99.5% gross profit margin** on every single visitor call.
