# HOA Pricing Hybrid Strategy: Base + Usage-Based Metering

This document outlines the **Base + Usage-Based Hybrid Pricing Strategy**, which solves the small HOA vs. large HOA profitability dilemma.

---

## ⚖️ The Problem with Single Pricing Strategies

1. **Pure Flat Monthly Fee Problem:**
   - A small 40-home HOA paying $349/mo is happy.
   - A large 500-home HOA paying $349/mo generates 3,500 calls/mo. You leave thousands of dollars in usage revenue on the table!
2. **Pure Per-Call Fee Problem:**
   - A large HOA generating 2,000 calls @ $1.00/call = $2,000/mo (Great profit!).
   - A small HOA generating 50 calls @ $1.00/call = $50/mo. You lose money on base infrastructure and admin support!

---

## 📝 Tier Specifications & Economics

### Tier 1: Starter Tier — $149 / month ($1,788 ARR)
* **Target:** Small subdivisions or boutique condos (< 80 units).
* **Included Allocation:** Up to 100 visitor calls / month.
* **Overage Rate:** **$2.00 per extra call**.
* **Financial Result:** Guaranteed minimum **$1,612 net annual profit** per site, even if the HOA is mostly idle.

### Tier 2: Growth Tier — $249 / month ($2,988 ARR)
* **Target:** Mid-sized residential HOAs (80 to 250 units).
* **Included Allocation:** Up to 500 visitor calls / month.
* **Overage Rate:** **$1.50 per extra call**.
* **Example (700 calls/mo):**
  - Base Fee: $249.00
  - Overages: 200 calls × $1.50 = $300.00
  - Total HOA Invoice: **$549.00 / month** ($6,588 / year)
  - Your COGS: 700 × $0.0147 = **$10.29 / month**
  - **Your Net Profit:** **$538.71 / month** (**98.1% Gross Margin**)

### Tier 3: Enterprise Tier — $499 / month ($5,988 ARR)
* **Target:** Master-planned developments (250+ units, multi-gate entrances).
* **Included Allocation:** Up to 1,500 visitor calls / month.
* **Overage Rate:** **$1.00 per extra call**.
* **Example (3,000 calls/mo):**
  - Base Fee: $499.00
  - Overages: 1,500 calls × $1.00 = $1,500.00
  - Total HOA Invoice: **$1,999.00 / month** ($23,988 / year)
  - Your COGS: 3,000 × $0.0147 = **$44.10 / month**
  - **Your Net Profit:** **$1,954.90 / month** (**97.8% Gross Margin**)
