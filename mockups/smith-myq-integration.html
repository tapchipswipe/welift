  • Tablet LiftMaster / myQ panel already installed — codes, directory, call button, camera
  • myQ Cloud: credentials, guest passes, activity log, remote unlock

2. Visitor paths (most never hit Smith)

Healthy economics = codes and guest passes do the work. Smith only handles exceptions.

1
No Smith call Resident / vendor types code or scans pass on tablet

myQ validates → gate opens. $0 to Smith. This should be the majority of traffic.

2
No Smith call Visitor rings a resident via directory

Resident answers in myQ app, sees video, unlocks. Still $0 to Smith.

3
Smith.ai call · $1.60 Visitor taps “Call attendant” (or resident doesn’t answer)

After hours, tablet SIP/phone destination is your Smith.ai number. Agent greets with community name and runs your script.

4
Verify Smith collects name, address, who they’re visiting

Checks against guest list you provide (portal, shared sheet, or tools). Ambiguous → deny or escalate to you by SMS.

5
Open gate Approved → unlock via myQ

Smith (or your automation) uses a myQ manager remote-open, or a dealer-set relay. Same unlock the property manager uses today.

6
Logged Visit logged for the board

Smith call notes + myQ activity = monthly PDF. Tablet stays the hardware of record.

4. Who does the “open” click

Three workable patterns — pick based on how much you trust Smith on day one.

Pattern How open happens Best for
A · Shadow (week 1–2) Smith verifies → texts you → you remote-open in myQ First HOA trial, lowest risk
B · Smith opens Smith has myQ manager login / approved unlock workflow After clean nights; still your post orders
C · Auto on match Exact guest-list match triggers unlock API/relay; Smith only for misses Scale; needs tighter integration
